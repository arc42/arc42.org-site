    <p class="dl-hero__sub">arc42 answers two questions in a pragmatic way: <strong>what</strong> should you document and communicate about your architecture, and <strong>how</strong>. Twelve sections, each with a clear purpose, tailorable to your specific needs.</p>
    <p class="dl-hero__facts">Open source and free, also for commercial use. Proven in practice since 2005, used by teams worldwide.</p>
