## In book form

![arc42 by Example, 3rd edition book cover](/images/books/arc42-by-example-3rd.webp){: .align-left .bookcover}

**arc42 by Example, 3rd edition** contains several complete real-world architectures, documented with arc42: a chess engine, a huge CRM system, a web system tracking biking activities, and an extremely small menu-bar application. Written by Gernot Starke, Michael Simons, Stefan Zörner, Ralf D. Müller and Hendrik Lösch.

Each example starts from the problem domain and quality requirements, walks through context, solution strategy, building blocks and runtime scenarios, and explains the crosscutting concepts of the architecture and its implementation.

<a href="https://leanpub.com/arc42byexample" class="btn btn--arc42-outline" rel="noopener">Read on Leanpub</a>

For *embedded systems and IoT*, [arc42 by Example Volume 2](/resources/?type=book#arc42-by-example-vol2) by Peter Hruschka, Ivan Kostov and Wolfgang Reimesch adds two documented hardware/software co-design architectures.
