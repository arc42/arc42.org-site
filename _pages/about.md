<section id="origin" class="about-section about-origin">
  <div class="about-section__heading">
    <p>Where it started</p>
    <h2>A small initiative with a long life</h2>
  </div>
  <div class="about-origin__story">
    <p>Peter Hruschka and Gernot Starke created arc42 after seeing the same problem in very different projects. Teams either had no architecture documentation or produced far too much of the wrong kind. A shared structure could remove arguments about form and let architects concentrate on useful content.</p>
    <p>The template has remained deliberately pragmatic: technology-independent, process-agnostic and adaptable to anything from embedded systems to large information platforms. It is free to use, including for commercial work.</p>
  </div>
  <ol class="about-timeline" aria-label="Selected arc42 milestones">
    <li><strong>2005</strong><span>Peter and Gernot publish the first version of arc42.</span></li>
    <li><strong>Open source</strong><span>The template becomes freely reusable and adaptable.</span></li>
    <li><strong>12 languages</strong><span>Translators make arc42 available to teams around the world.</span></li>
    <li><strong>Today</strong><span>Template, method, canvas, examples, tools and training form a living ecosystem.</span></li>
  </ol>
</section>
