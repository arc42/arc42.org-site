# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Software architects, software developers, technical leaders, and system engineers. They operate in fast-paced engineering environments where documenting software architecture is critical but must remain pragmatic, lightweight, and structured to prevent overhead.

## Product Purpose
To serve as the definitive home for the arc42 software architecture template. The site exists to make downloading the template in various formats (e.g., Markdown, AsciiDoc, Word) as frictionless as possible, educate users on the arc42 methodology through practical examples and books, and promote professional training workshops.

## Brand Personality
* **Pragmatic**: Grounded in real-world experience; focuses on what works rather than academic theory.
* **Structured**: Organized, clean, and logical—just like the arc42 template sections.
* **Professional**: Authoritative, trustworthy, and clear, speaking directly to experienced engineers.

## Anti-references
* **Over-decorated SaaS Clichés**: No random decorative grid backgrounds, gradient text, tiny tracked uppercase kicker eyebrows on every section, or floaty glassmorphism.
* **Low-Contrast Aesthetics**: No muted light-gray text on near-white backgrounds that compromises readability.
* **Unnecessary Motion**: No scroll-jacking or heavy entry animations that slow down access to downloads or documentation.

## Design Principles
1. **Structure is the Message**: The site design should reflect the structured clarity of the arc42 documentation template itself. The content hierarchy must be immediately clear and easily scannable.
2. **Frictionless Utility**: Prioritize direct access to templates, examples, and docs. Developers should be able to get what they came for within seconds of landing.
3. **Expert Confidence**: Speak with the authority of proven engineering practices. Use crisp, high-contrast typography and clear layout rhythm to build immediate trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* Target WCAG AA compliance with high-contrast ratios (body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1).
* Full support for user preferences, including reduced motion.
* Mobile-responsive layout that maintains typographic integrity and prevents horizontal scroll or overflow on small viewports.
