*arc42 in Aktion* is the missing manual for arc42, the book we probably should have
written years ago. It collects pragmatic, hands-on tips for architecture
documentation: starting from the general foundations of good technical documentation,
right through to concrete advice for every part of arc42.

## What's inside

- Practical guidance for creating documentation that is useful, economical and
  maintainable, not documentation for its own sake.
- Worked examples, including a substantial second example from the world of
  **real-time and embedded systems**.
- Expanded coverage of tooling, refined tips throughout, and a generally tidied-up,
  more complete treatment compared with earlier editions.

## Who it is for

Anyone using arc42 in practice who wants to write less, communicate more, and keep
their architecture documentation genuinely maintainable.
