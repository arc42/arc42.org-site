03 / The working method

A template is useful only when people use it.

arc42 is iterative, feedback-driven, and process-agnostic. Teams can use it with agile delivery, formal governance, legacy modernization, or a small product that needs only a few pages.

The structure remains stable while the level of detail changes. That stability makes architecture information easier to compare across systems and easier for new team members to learn.
