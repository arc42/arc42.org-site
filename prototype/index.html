arc42.org redesign study

Seven ways to make structure feel human.

Each proposal is a linked static prototype. Review the homepage first, then inspect Download, Method, and Resources to see whether the direction survives real content. V4 through V7 follow the simplified 2026 information architecture.

How to review

  1. Ignore whether a single hero looks attractive. Ask whether the visual grammar still works on Download and Resources.
  2. Check whether people feel integrated into the work or merely added as decoration.
  3. Compare mobile navigation and content density.
  4. Note which elements should be combined across proposals.
