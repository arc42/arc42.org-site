Not sure which format?

Take AsciiDoc if your docs live in version control next to the code. Take Word if your organization reviews documents by mail. Take Markdown if your wiki renders it. There is no wrong answer: the structure is identical everywhere, and switching later is cheap.

Free means free. arc42 is open source and free of charge, in commercial and private settings. No attribution walls, no premium tier.
