What is arc42?

arc42 is based on practical experience with many systems across domains: information and web systems, real-time and embedded, business intelligence and data warehouses.

It gives you a template for the documentation and communication of software and system architecture: twelve sections, each with a clear purpose, tailorable to your specific needs.

  • Supports arbitrary technologies and tools, with nothing to install.
  • Process-agnostic, and especially well-suited to lean and agile teams.
  • Open source and free of charge, in commercial and private settings.

The template answers where architecture information belongs. The arc42 method answers how you work to produce it.

Two authors, twenty years, one template

arc42 was created by Dr. Peter Hruschka and Dr. Gernot Starke, and has been maintained, taught and battle-tested ever since. It grew out of workshops, reviews and real systems, not out of theory.

They still teach it today, in iSAQB-certified architecture trainings, on-site and remote, alongside a shelf of books, articles, talks and videos.
